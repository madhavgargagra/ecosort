🎮 Sort It! Game

Sort waste items correctly and earn Eco Credits — pick your mode!

🗑️

Drag & Drop

Waste items fall from the top. Drag them into the correct bin before they hit the floor! You have 3 lives — don't let items fall into the wrong bin.

  • ⏱️ 60-second rounds
  • ❤️ 3 lives per round
  • 🔥 Combo multipliers
  • 🪙 +5 credits per correct sort
⚡

Speed Quiz

A waste item appears on screen. Tap the correct bin as fast as possible! Answer faster for a higher speed multiplier.

  • 🎯 15 items per round
  • ⚡ Speed multiplier up to 3×
  • 💡 Hint system (costs points)
  • 🪙 +3–9 credits per correct answer
